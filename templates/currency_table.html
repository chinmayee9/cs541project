The ratings of the currencies are based on the following parameters:

  • Popularity(%) - This is calculated using the crypto news database. It is quantified by the frequency of mentions in the news articles.
  • Market Capital Dominance(%) - This is the share of the currency in global crypto market.
  • Volatility(%) - This is quantified by the percent spread of 90-days closing prices of the currency about the mean. Learn more
  • Skewness - The skewness is a measure of symmetry 90-days closing prices about their mean. Learn more
  • Kurtosis - The kurtosis is a measure of likelihood of extreme outcomes. Learn more
